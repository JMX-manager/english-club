About Us

More Than a Club

The English Club at Fushun No.1 Senior High is a student-led space where we drop the textbooks and actually speak. From movie nights to debate sessions, we create real opportunities to use English in ways that feel natural and fun.

No test prep. No grades. Just people who want to get better at expressing themselves in another language.

What We Do

Regular Activities

Something for everyone — from casual to competitive.

🎬

Movie Nights

Watch English films with subtitles, then discuss. Casual and fun.

🎤

Speech & Debate

Practice presenting and arguing — in English — on topics you care about.

📖

Reading Circles

Short stories, articles, poems. Read together, talk about what they mean.
